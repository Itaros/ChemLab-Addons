All items are named using tree-like structure separating nodes by point:
ore.crushed.bif
ingot.pig.lowgrade.bif

First group is visual type: ore, grains, slag, ingot and so on.
Second one is visual modifier: crushed, rich, pig, nonmagnetic or none at all.
Third one is material: bif(Banded Iron Formation), hematite etc
Third one might have a quality prefix like .lowgrade.
